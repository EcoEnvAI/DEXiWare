About

Pathfinder is a decision support system for assessment and management of the sustainability of legume agri-food chains.

It was developed within the project True (“TRansition paths to sUstainable legume based systems in Europe ”) co-funded by the Horizon 2020 Programme of the European Union.

Pathfinder is powered by DEXiWare, a web application built around DEXi, a computer program for multi-attribute decision making. DEXi is aimed at interactive development of qualitative multi-attribute decision models and the evaluation of options. With DEXiWare, these models are available to end-users online.

For more information contact us at dexiware@ijs.si
